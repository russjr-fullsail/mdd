About Me

I got into web designing back in the late 90's. I found it interesting. I started out using HTML all the time to do it. Then when software like Frontpage came out, I switched to that. Over the years I have lost most of my HTML programming skills. I finally decided it was time to get back into it and learn how to do it the right way. This led me to Full Sail University. I still am having trouble grasping the concepts, but I know as I practice I will get better. I am really enjoying the experience I am getting also. I have learned some really great things.
